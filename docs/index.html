Privacy Bulk Actions for Caflou

A Chrome extension for bulk management of privacy rights in Caflou CRM.

EN | CZ

About the Extension

This extension adds helpful bulk action buttons to the "Privacy" tab within business cases in Caflou. It allows you to quickly check, uncheck, or restore privacy settings for all users at once, saving you valuable time.

Key Features:

  • Clear All: Instantly remove all rights across all pages for all users.
  • Restore All: Revert the rights to default role-based settings for all users.
  • Grant All: Quickly assign Read, Write, and Health rights to everyone.

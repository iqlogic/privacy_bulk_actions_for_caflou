Privacy Policy

Privacy Bulk Actions for Caflou

EN | CZ

Last Updated: May 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Privacy Bulk Actions for Caflou" Chrome extension ("the Extension") handles information. We take your privacy seriously and have designed this Extension to be completely privacy-respecting.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The Extension operates entirely locally within your web browser (client-side). It interacts directly with the Caflou web application's Document Object Model (DOM) to automate clicking actions on checkboxes related to privacy settings.

No analytics, tracking, telemetry, or user data of any kind is sent to our servers or any third-party servers.

3. Permissions

The Extension requires the following permission to function:

  • Host Permission (*://*.caflou.cz/*, *://*.caflou.com/*): Required to inject the automation script strictly and only into Caflou's website to interact with the privacy settings interface.

4. Third-Party Services

Since the Extension does not collect any data, no data is shared with or sold to third-party services.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

6. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in our GitHub repository.
